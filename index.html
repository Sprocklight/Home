Case Study

Acquired 2021, SkinCartel is a premium scented skincare line in Canada that sells products mainly online. They had the business vision, but no brand. Our task was to build an identity, launch a website, and bring this all-new moisturizer to market.

Website Layout + Design

A bright, modern website that stands out in the space, infuses insights gleaned from our market research and puts our audience first with client-centric design.

Logo + Branding

A controversial name (Cartel, due to increasing popularity of drug tv series), a modern logo and fresh design elements, and a conversational, yet bold brand voice.

Case Study

The Emerald Events Place is a luxurious venue situated in Antipolo City, that offers a majestic view of the Metro Manila skyline from Makati, to BGC, to Ortigas, as well as both Laguna de Bay and Manila Bay. Their goal is to have an all-in-one website wherein clients can book their events directly, without needing to meet up in person. With a site that had never been optimized for SEO, they wanted their site to show at the top search for "Antipolo Venue" keyword.

Website Layout + Design

A minimalist approach on their website that resonates their brand, along with a booking system that is fully integrated with their company email to minimize operating expenses.

SEO Marketing

We first made sure the site content was set up for success by implementing our keywords in meta titles and descriptions, title tags, and in the content on specific target pages. We continued this process for a few months, resulting in even more organic traffic and higher rankings. The continuous link-building, website audit, and individual location pages helped drive positive results.

Case Study

Propeller Realty is a Real Estate Brokerage based in Metro Manila that specializes in Residential and Commercial Properties for Sale and Lease. Their goal is to have online visibility wherein clients can view all their listings, and to request for quotations and other services. They want to ensure that their website is optimized on both mobile and desktop users.

Website Layout + Design

A website that resonates their sleekness, showcasing their listings that automatically creates dedicated property pages. We have also integrated product filters, and automatic collection pages based on the property's city/location to reduce workload.

Search Engine Optimization

Search engine optimization allows your business or brand to appear when a relevant search term is “Googled”, allowing you to control who finds you and when. Below are some of the ways we can help you advertise your business using SEO tactics:

  • Research

    • Substantial research is the key to creating a successful SEO strategy. It’s important to outline keywords, search terms, trends, and understand the competitive landscape.

  • On-Page SEO

    • Optimizing websites for search engines by editing backend tags, optimizing images and videos and other content is everything - if search engines can’t read your content or site then your business will struggle to launch a successful SEO campaign.

  • Off-Page SEO

    • Using external sources in the form of outreach strategies can be beneficial for your business and/or brand. Getting recognized by other platforms, sites, and social influencers can define true page relevancy.
